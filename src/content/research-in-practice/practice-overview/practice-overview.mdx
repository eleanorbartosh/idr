### Establish a shared vocabulary, and align your team on the urgency and need for IBM Design Research. 

## Drive innovation through evidence and storytelling

The practice of IBM Design Research is facilitated by a Guide, or the people on your team trained in performing research. Guides help your team understand how to craft good learning objectives, become a better moderator, ensure the research remains unbiased, and much more.
